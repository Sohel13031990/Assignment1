Take.Ride.Return

From station to station…
PADDLE is Dhaka’s bike sharing system. Take one when you need one, and leave it at any station. Our Service is Available at Gulshan,Banani,Baridhara, Bashundhara and Uttara.when you arrive at your destination For a trip on the fly or as part of your daily routine, go with paddle and save your time from unavoided traffic jam.

Take

Go to the pay station, choose the bike icon, buy your day pass, one-way trip, or 10 One way package, then read and accept the user contract. Want to use PADDLE more often? Get a membership and take advantage of PADDLE for your daily trips. A valid credit card must be associated to your account at all times!

Ride

Adjust the seat height, put your bag in the basket and ride! The 30/45 minutes included in your purchase start now! Using the PADDLE app, you can plan your trips and view a map of the stations. You can also locate available docking points at the stations closest to your destination.Need to check the map while en route? Be safe and make sure that you find a good place to stop. Download the free mobile app from the App Store or Google Play.

Return

Return the bike to any of the 540 BIXI stations. Push the front wheel firmly into an empty bike dock. IMPORTANT: Make sure the green light comes on after docking the bike. You have to wait 1 minutes before taking out another bike.To get a new unlocking code, insert your credit card again: the system will recognize it and no additional fees will be charged to your card if you make trips under 30 minutes.
